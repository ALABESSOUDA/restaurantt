OJJA MERGUEZZ

Tunisian ojja is a dish of merguez and eggs, bound by a tasty tomato sauce. Quick and easy to make, Tunisian ojja is a perfect express recipe for cooking beginners. It can be served with semolina or pasta for a balanced meal. But traditionally, it is eaten with bread. Try it, you will definitely delight the taste buds of young and old alike.

BRIK B THON

These tuna briks (or tuna bricks) are one of the emblematic recipes of traditional Tunisian cuisine. The other name for brik leaf is malsouka. Malsouka, of Tunisian origin, is a very thin pastry that is semi-cooked. Briks are preparations that can be available in several forms. They are often part of iftar tables, which is the meal taken by Muslims at sunset during the month of Ramadan.
